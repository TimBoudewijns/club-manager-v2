Holding the Stick

Proper Stick Handling

Before mastering advanced techniques, it's crucial to understand proper stick handling:

  • Left Hand: Firm grip at the top of the stick, with fingers wrapped around (no penholder grip)
  • Right Hand: Positioned in the middle, held loosely
  • Posture: Arms extended, stick in front, elbows apart

Turning the Stick

  • Grip: Start with hands in normal grip
  • Movement: Rotate stick by twisting wrists clockwise or counterclockwise
  • Purpose: Quick transitions between forehand and backhand
  • Execution: Keep stick close to ground during turn

Running with the Ball

Running with the Ball (Dribbling)

  • Body Position: Bend knees with upright upper body and straight back
  • Grip: Hold stick in normal grip
  • Ball Position: Keep ball in front and slightly right of body
  • Stick Angle: Hold at 45Âº angle above ground
  • Vision: Use split vision on ball and field
  • Movement: Stay balanced and in control

Stopping the Ball from a Dribble

  • Stick Movement: Flip stick so rounded side faces forward
  • Positioning: Place stick in front of ball with space between ball and feet
  • Contact: Use flat side to cushion ball's momentum
  • Follow-Up: Be prepared for quick action left or right

Indian Dribble

  • Grip: Normal grip, elbows apart
  • Body Movement: Minimize upper body rotation
  • Stick Position: Keep over ball with rhythmic movements
  • Ball Control: Move ball left to right in front of body
  • Vision: Maintain split vision

Eccentric Dribble

  • Ball Position: Move ball outside body line, left or right
  • Grip: Maintain normal grip with hands apart
  • Control: Keep ball further from body than regular dribble
  • Stick Position: Focus on keeping stick low to ground
  • Movement: Quick, a-rhythmic movements to throw off defenders

A-Rhythmic Dribble

  • Ball Movement: Alternate between rhythmic and irregular patterns
  • Grip: Normal grip for flexibility
  • Direction: Take ball outside body left, then switch right
  • Execution: Mix up speed and direction
  • Repetition: Continuously alternate movements

Normal Slalom (Backhand-Forehand)

  • Grip: Normal grip
  • Ball Position: Keep far from feet for control
  • Hand Movement: Rotate left hand inward for backhand
  • Execution: Move ball side-to-side alternating backhand/forehand
  • Awareness: Keep split vision

Normal Slalom (Forehand Only)

  • Grip: Maintain normal grip
  • Ball Position: Keep at distance from feet
  • Turning Right: Point left elbow forward
  • Footwork: Use small steps to "walk around ball"
  • Execution: Continuously move ball side to side using only forehand

Running with Direction and Tempo Changes

  • Grip: Normal grip with hands apart
  • Elbow Position: Keep elbows apart for balance
  • Ball Control: Maintain constant contact
  • Direction Change: Pull ball left or "walk around" right
  • Tempo Shift: Accelerate quickly after direction change

Running with an Open Forehand

  • Ball Position: Keep to right of body, near back foot
  • Stick Technique: Open flat side with each contact
  • Passing Option: Keep ball ready to pass right anytime
  • Execution: Run while maintaining control and passing options

One-Handed Dribbling (Left and Right)

  • Arm Position: Extend arm fully for increased reach
  • Wrist Action: Bend wrist inward toward body
  • Ball Control: Dribble with one hand to keep ball away
  • Body Position: Shield ball with body from defender
  • Transition: Recover ball to forehand with two hands

Passing the Ball

Sweep Pass

  • Grip: Normal grip, hands apart
  • Stick Position: Place on ground near right foot
  • Ball Position: Keep level with left foot
  • Body Position: Point left shoulder in direction of play
  • Movement: Slide stick backward, then sweep forward through ball
  • Follow Through: Point stick towards ball after passing

Sweep Pass While Running (Rolling Ball)

  • Grip: Keep hands apart
  • Body Position: Turn left shoulder towards target
  • Stick Movement: Sweep from back to front along ground
  • Posture: Bend knees, stick almost horizontal
  • Follow Through: Direct ball by pointing stick after pass

Long Slap/Sweep Pass

  • Technique: Combine upper body rotation, arm swing, wrist action
  • Ball Position: At or just ahead of left foot, away from body
  • Grip: Both hands together at top of stick
  • Movement: Rotate upper body with wide sweeping motion
  • Follow Through: Shift weight from back to front leg

Backhand Long Slap/Sweep Pass

  • Ball Position: Near or in front of right foot, away from body
  • Grip: Hands together at top, arms free
  • Movement: Sweep stick in wide semicircle on ground
  • Body Movement: Rotate upper body, shift weight
  • Follow Through: Wrist movement determines direction

Push Pass

  • Grip: Normal grip, hands apart
  • Body Position: Left shoulder faces target, knees bent
  • Ball Position: In front of body, right of right foot
  • Movement: Pull stick back, then push ball over ground
  • Follow Through: Shift weight to left foot

Hit/Long Forehand Pass

  • Ball Position: At or just ahead of left foot
  • Grip: Hands together at top of stick
  • Posture: Slightly bend knees and upper body
  • Swing: Bring stick back, stretch arms during hit
  • Follow Through: Shift weight back to front, rotate body

High Push (Scoop)

  • Grip: Normal grip with elbows apart
  • Ball Position: In front of body, away from feet
  • Movement: Bend knees deeply, place stick diagonally under ball
  • Leverage: Push with right hand, pull with left
  • Follow Through: Keep contact as long as possible, point upwards

Short Slap Shot

  • Ball Position: About 40-50 cm in front
  • Grip: Hands together, slightly below top for shortened grip
  • Movement: Quick wrist movement, short backswing
  • Posture: Weight on left leg, left shoulder above knee
  • Follow Through: Squeeze stick at contact, point towards ball

Lifting the Ball

  • Ball Position: Slightly in front of feet
  • Stick Angle: Diagonally under ball, flat side up
  • Execution: Quick wrist action to lift ball
  • Follow-Up: Keep contact for controlled elevation

One-Touch Pass

  • Posture: Bend knees, lean forward, extend arms
  • Movement: Pass with single hit, no backswing
  • Direction: Shoulder and flat side guide direction

Chop

  • Ball Position: Behind or near right foot
  • Stick Technique: Sharp, quick downward motion
  • Execution: Chop ball to make it jump/lift
  • Follow-Up: Accelerate quickly after chop

Fake Pass

  • Purpose: Deceive opponent and create space
  • Setup: Approach ball as if passing
  • Execution: Make convincing motion without contact
  • Body Movement: Fake in pass direction, then redirect
  • Follow-Up: Use created space to advance

Receiving the Ball

Forehand Receiving

  • Stance: Feet slightly apart, left foot ahead
  • Posture: Bend knees, arms free, left arm "checking watch"
  • Stick: Hold loosely with right hand, vertical
  • Ball Control: Stop in front of right foot

Dynamic Forehand Receiving

  • Movement: Approach ball with stick close to ground
  • Stick Position: Flat side in front to cover ball
  • Body Position: Push right shoulder down
  • Guidance: Guide ball diagonally in front of right foot

Forehand Receiving to Backhand Dribble

  • Movement: Receive diagonally in front of right foot
  • Transition: Use left elbow to guide to left foot
  • Wrist Action: Rotate right wrist, elbow points outward
  • Result: Transition to backhand dribbling

Forehand Open Receiving

  • Body Movement: Move left to create space
  • Ball Control: Let ball roll past body
  • Stick Position: Keep flat side open
  • Follow-Up: Quick decision to pass or accelerate

Backhand Open Receiving

  • Body Position: Align body for backhand
  • Stick Rotation: Rotate early, flat side ready
  • Ball Control: Let ball roll past body
  • Distance: Keep ball away from body
  • Follow-Up: Transition to dribble, pass, or shot

Receiving from the Left

  • Stick Angle: Position at 45Âº to ground
  • Ball Control: Receive near left foot, use soft hands
  • Body Movement: Turn left elbow away
  • Follow-Up: Quickly bring to forehand dribble

Receiving from the Right (Backhand in Front)

  • Stick Rotation: Hook points down, flat side ready
  • Stick Position: Hold at 45Âº angle
  • Elbow Movement: Right elbow points toward ball
  • Ball Control: Receive in front of left foot
  • Follow-Up: Guide to normal dribble position

Receiving from the Right (Back Foot)

  • Body Position: Turn upper body right
  • Stick Position: 45Âº angle, flat side open
  • Ball Control: Receive near back foot
  • Follow-Up: Guide to forehand dribble

Closed Receiving

  • Purpose: Shield ball from opponents
  • Body Position: Face away from opponent's goal
  • Stick Position: Ball in front, elbows apart
  • Execution: Receive with back to opponent
  • Follow-Up: Ready to pivot or pass

Receiving and Turning

  • Initial Control: Receive on forehand while shielding
  • Body Movement: Pivot on feet and turn quickly
  • Stick Control: Maintain close contact with ball
  • Follow-Up: Accelerate out of turn

Side-Stop

  • Feint Movement: Step towards ball initially
  • Execution: Step back, let ball roll in front
  • Stick Position: Stop ball while turning shoulder forward
  • Follow-Up: Quickly lift or accelerate past defender

Aerial Receiving

  • Stance: Feet shoulder-width, knees bent
  • Stick Position: Vertical with flat side up
  • Ball Control: Watch closely, cushion with soft hands
  • Execution: Contact slightly above ground
  • Follow-Up: Smooth transition to next move

Passing the Defender

Haringtrick (Dutch Move)

  • Approach: Dribble towards opponent, feint forward
  • Execution: Pass ball to opponent's right
  • Movement: Quickly move to their left
  • Follow-Up: Regain possession and continue

Chop (Passing Technique)

  • Setup: Position ball near/behind right foot
  • Action: As opponent moves, hit quickly
  • Result: Ball jumps over their stick
  • Follow-Up: Accelerate past opponent

Receiving Lift

  • Body Position: Balanced stance, knees bent
  • Stick Angle: Flat side up at 45Âº
  • Execution: Receive softly, absorb momentum
  • Follow-Up: Quick transition to dribble

Drag/Dummy (Left)

  • Body Position: Approach low, knees bent
  • Stick Control: Drag ball left with flat side
  • Fake Movement: Pretend to continue left
  • Execution: Change direction at last moment

Drag/Dummy (Right)

  • Body Position: Approach low with knees bent
  • Stick Control: Drag ball to right
  • Fake Movement: Pretend to continue right
  • Execution: Quick direction change or pass

Triangle Pass (Right)

  • Setup: Form triangle with teammates
  • Execution: Pass ball right with flat side
  • Movement: Move into space after passing
  • Purpose: Maintain possession and create space

Shuffle

  • Body Position: Stay low, knees bent
  • Stick Control: Keep ball close with soft hands
  • Execution: Rapid left-right movements
  • Footwork: Quick, light steps in sync
  • Purpose: Maintain possession in tight spaces

Dragflick Turn

  • Approach: Ball in front, feet positioned for pivot
  • Body Rotation: Rotate quickly, ball on stick
  • Stick Movement: Slide under ball, push forward/upward
  • Execution: Flick with wrist snap while turning
  • Follow-Up: Shift weight forward, continue moving

Defending

Tackle Back

  • Approach: Move from behind or side
  • Timing: Wait until beside opponent
  • Execution: Hit ball off opponent's stick
  • Additional: Can use jab, steal, or shave

Frontal Block (Forehand)

  • Approach: Let opponent approach on forehand
  • Posture: Bend deeply at knees, weight forward
  • Stick Position: Horizontal in left hand, arm extended
  • Block: Block in middle of stick, push forward/left
  • Follow-Up: Place right hand on stick for next action

Shave Tackle

  • Approach: Move from behind opponent
  • Stick Position: Left hand, flat side up
  • Execution: Place stick between ball and feet
  • Movement: Quick wrist to shave ball away
  • Follow-Up: Pull ball towards you

Steal

  • Approach: Move from behind
  • Stick Position: Flat side half-open, hook down
  • Execution: Place over opponent's stick
  • Movement: Flick ball right with wrist
  • Follow-Up: Pull ball towards you

Jab on Forehand

  • Technique: One-handed move
  • Stick Position: Left hand, flat side up
  • Reach: Extend left arm and front foot
  • Execution: Stab ball off opponent's stick
  • Follow-Up: Step back to avoid being passed

Jab as a Tackle Back Technique

  • Approach: Close in from side/behind
  • Stick Position: Left hand, flat side up
  • Execution: Extend arm, jab quickly
  • Contact: Minimal contact to stab ball away
  • Follow-Up: Step back after jab

Jab Dribble

  • Grip: Stick in left hand, arm extended
  • Wrist Action: Quick stabbing motion
  • Ball Control: Keep ball slightly elevated
  • Purpose: Maintain possession while moving past defenders

Scoring Techniques

Hitting While Turning

  • Setup: Balanced stance for quick pivot
  • Body Movement: Rotate around planted foot
  • Stick Position: Maintain control during turn
  • Execution: Time hit as turn completes
  • Follow-Up: Maintain balance for next action

Hitting While Running

  • Grip: Maintain firm grip while running
  • Body Balance: Stay low and balanced
  • Timing: Coordinate steps with hit
  • Execution: Controlled swing maintaining momentum
  • Follow-Through: Continue running after hit

Hitting from the Right Leg

  • Stance: Weight on right leg
  • Ball Placement: Front and right of right foot
  • Body Rotation: Rotate upper body for power
  • Swing: Controlled but powerful
  • Follow-Through: Smooth for balance and accuracy

Low Long Slap

  • Body Position: Bend knees, lower body
  • Stick Grip: Both hands close to top
  • Ball Position: Front and to side
  • Swing: Wide, low sweeping motion
  • Follow-Through: Continue for accuracy and distance

High Long Slap

  • Stance: Lower body, knees bent
  • Grip: Hands together near top
  • Ball Position: Front and away from body
  • Swing: Long sweep, lift during follow-through
  • Follow-Through: Upward motion for elevation

Backhand Long Slap

  • Stance: Side-on, knees bent
  • Grip: Both hands near top
  • Ball Position: Near/in front of right foot
  • Stick Motion: Wide sweep on backhand
  • Follow-Through: Smooth power transfer

Slapshot

  • Stance: Knees bent, feet shoulder-width
  • Grip: Firm with both hands near top
  • Ball Position: Slightly in front, within reach
  • Execution: Short backswing, quick snap
  • Follow-Through: Extend forward, keep low and fast

Chip/Chap Slap

  • Stance: Knees bent for balance
  • Grip: Both hands near top
  • Ball Position: Close to body
  • Execution: Quick, sharp slap with short backswing
  • Follow-Through: Short for quick recovery

Drag Flick

  • Stance: Low position, knees bent
  • Ball Placement: Ahead of front foot
  • Grip: Firm, hands slightly apart
  • Execution: Drag ball, rotate body and wrists
  • Follow-Through: Continue upwards for power

Tip-In

  • Positioning: Stand near goal
  • Stick Control: Keep low, flat side ready
  • Execution: Gentle tap to redirect
  • Timing: Precise to meet incoming ball

Reverse Flick

  • Stance: Side-on to ball
  • Stick Grip: Firm with both hands
  • Ball Position: Front and left
  • Execution: Rotate wrists, scoop motion
  • Follow-Up: Snap wrists at contact

Penalty Stroke

  • Stance: Behind ball, feet apart
  • Ball Placement: On penalty spot
  • Stick Grip: Firm with both hands
  • Execution: Short backswing, push or flick
  • Follow-Through: Extend, aim for corners

1v1 with Goalkeeper

  • Approach: Control ball while assessing keeper
  • Body Position: Low, balanced stance
  • Decision Making: Choose shoot, dribble, or fake
  • Execution: Use feints and speed changes
  • Follow-Up: Quick controlled finish

High Reverse Backhand

  • Stance: Slightly side-on, feet apart
  • Grip: Firm, stick in reverse position
  • Ball Position: Front and left of body
  • Execution: Swing low to high, wrist flick
  • Follow-Through: Extended, eyes on target
