Our Story

At Dutch Field Hockey Drills, we’re driven by a passion to empower field hockey trainers with the best coaching resources available.

Our journey began when we noticed a gap in the hockey community: while many coaches were dedicated to improving their players, the tools and resources available were often either too expensive, difficult to use, or too limited in scope.

Built by coaches, for coaches

Having spent years working with various hockey clubs, we saw firsthand how challenging it was for trainers to find affordable, comprehensive, and easy-to-understand drills. That’s when we realized we could make a difference. As a team of experienced and enthusiastic hockey experts, we came together with a mission—to revolutionize how field hockey training is done.

We built Dutch Field Hockey Drills to offer exactly what we wished had existed during our own coaching careers: an accessible, extensive, and budget-friendly database of high-quality hockey drills, designed for trainers at all levels. Whether you’re working with youth teams or professional players, our platform provides everything you need to succeed.

We’re not just about drills; we’re about elevating the entire coaching experience. From structured training plans to a personalized drill configurator, we ensure that every trainer has the tools to bring out the best in their players. With a focus on simplicity, flexibility, and real-world results, Dutch Field Hockey Drills is your partner in coaching success.

Today, we’re proud to serve coaches and trainers worldwide, making field hockey training more effective, more fun, and more accessible than ever before. We invite you to join us and discover how Dutch Field Hockey Drills can transform your coaching approach.

Join us and be part of a growing community that’s raising the standard of field hockey training!
